# Privacy Policy for Pixel Wall

**Last updated:** November 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when You use the Pixel Wall app.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Pixel Wall is a "client-only" application. It does not have a server or database. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect or store any of your personal data.

* **Favorites:** Your "favorites" list is stored locally on your device's internal storage. We do not have access to this list, and it is never uploaded or shared.
* **Settings:** Your app settings (like theme) are stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

Pixel Wall acts as a client for the Unsplash API. All wallpapers and collection data are fetched directly from Unsplash. Your interaction with Unsplash is subject to their own policies:

* **Unsplash Privacy Policy:** https://unsplash.com/privacy
* **Unsplash Terms:** https://unsplash.com/terms

We do not send any personal data to Unsplash.

## Permissions

Pixel Wall requests the following permissions on your Android device to function:

* `android.permission.INTERNET`: To connect to the Unsplash API and fetch wallpapers.
* `android.permission.SET_WALLPAPER`: To allow you to apply a wallpaper to your home screen or lock screen.
* `android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` / `android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`: To read image files when applying a wallpaper.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
